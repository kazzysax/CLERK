THE SUPPORT LAYER

A support agent, paid on outcomes, not attention.

Clerk plugs into your ticket queue, drafts answers grounded only in your own docs, and never guesses when it isn't sure — every part of that is enforced, not promised.

✦

Resolve or escalate — never guess

Every draft carries a confidence score and its source. High confidence auto-sends. Anything shakier goes to your team with Clerk's draft attached, not a blind reply to your customer.

⛓

Pay per resolution, enforced onchain

Each answer locks its fee in escrow. A 72-hour reopen window has to pass with no dispute before Clerk gets paid — so "resolved" is a fact your money can check, not a claim.

★

Reputation you can verify, not take our word for

Every rating comes from real customers on real resolved tickets, scored with a confidence-weighted average onchain. Read it yourself — nothing here is self-reported.

UNDER THE HOOD

The parts most agents don't bother building.

Clerk isn't just a chatbot with a wallet. These are the pieces that make it trustworthy enough to pay automatically.

◎

Learns your tone before it ever speaks

Put Clerk in shadow mode and it watches your team handle real tickets — learning your voice, your judgment calls, what's easy versus what needs care. It can draft in your style before it's ever trusted to send.

✎

Knows when it's wrong more than it thinks

Clerk tracks whether its stated confidence actually matches how often it's right — per category, per merchant. If it's overconfident on refunds but great on shipping, the two get different bars automatically.

↻

Spots the pattern, not just the ticket

Fifteen people asking about the same shipping delay this week isn't fifteen problems — it's one. Clerk flags the repeat pattern so you fix the cause, and answers the rest with higher confidence.

★

Two ratings, and one can't fake the other

Customers and merchants both rate Clerk — but a merchant can't rate an interaction unless a real customer already did. No self-graded reputation, ever.

⏻

You can always get your money back

Even if Clerk's operator pauses everything, merchants can still reopen a bad resolution and withdraw their own escrow. That circuit breaker only ever stops new charges — never your exit.

⇄

No OKB? Swap for it, right in onboarding

Hold USDT or USDC instead? Clerk quotes a live swap into OKB through the OKX DEX aggregator — your wallet signs, Clerk never touches the funds.

HOW IT WORKS

From a new ticket to a settled payment.

01

A ticket arrives

Clerk reads it against your own docs and past tickets — nothing outside what you've taught it.

02

It drafts, scores, and decides

Confident enough → sends and locks its fee in escrow. Not sure → hands it to your team with the draft attached.

03

The customer can push back

A reply saying it didn't work auto-reopens the resolution — Clerk's own payout is voided, no dispute form needed.

04

The window closes, payment settles

No reopen in 72 hours → escrow releases and the resolution becomes part of Clerk's permanent, public track record.
